Frequently Asked Questions

How much does digital marketing cost?

Digital marketing costs vary based on your business goals, target audience, and chosen strategies. We offer customized packages tailored to meet your specific needs and budget. Our transparent pricing ensures you know exactly what you're paying for and the results you can expect. Most clients invest between $2,500 and $10,000 monthly depending on the scope and competitiveness of their industry.

How long does it take to see results from SEO?

SEO is a long-term strategy that typically takes 3-6 months to show significant results. However, many clients start seeing improvements within the first few months. The timeline depends on factors like your website's current condition, industry competition, and the aggressiveness of your strategy. We provide monthly reports showing your progress in rankings, traffic, and conversions.

Do I need social media for my business?

While not every business needs to be on every social platform, most businesses benefit from strategic social media presence. We help you identify which platforms your target audience uses most and develop a strategy that aligns with your business goals and resources. Social media is particularly valuable for building brand awareness, engaging with customers, and supporting other marketing channels.

How do you measure campaign success?

We measure success based on your specific business objectives, tracking key performance indicators (KPIs) such as traffic, conversions, leads, sales, and ROI. We provide comprehensive monthly reports that show exactly how your campaigns are performing and what adjustments we're making to optimize results. Our reporting dashboards give you 24/7 access to your campaign metrics, ensuring complete transparency.

What makes {{business_name}} different from other agencies?

We differentiate ourselves through our data-driven approach, transparent reporting, and focus on ROI. Unlike many agencies, we don't lock clients into long-term contracts; we earn your business each month through results. Our team consists of specialized experts in each digital marketing discipline, ensuring top-tier execution across all strategies. We also limit our client roster to ensure personalized service and attention to every account.
